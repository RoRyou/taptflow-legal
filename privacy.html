TaptFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

TaptFlow ("the App") is developed and operated by lu liang (individual developer). We value your privacy and collect as little data as possible.

What we collect

  • Anonymous account ID — created automatically on first launch (no name, email, or phone number), used only to store your own data.
  • Content you create — your flows, custom skills, documents uploaded to "My Knowledge", and Wishlist posts/votes. Stored on Supabase (cloud database). Each user can only access their own data.
  • Error logs — when a flow run fails, the error type/message is reported to improve the product. Your input content is not included.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or location.
  • No advertising SDKs, no third-party behavioral analytics.
  • We never sell or share your data.

Camera & microphone

  • Used only when you actively start a photo or voice flow.
  • Photos and speech are processed on your device by the built-in models; they are not uploaded by us.

On-device AI vs. cloud AI

  • On-device model (default): AI runs on your device; your prompts and photos never leave your phone.
  • Cloud AI: if you choose a cloud model or add your own API key, the content of that request is sent to the corresponding provider (e.g. DeepSeek) to generate a result. We do not store this content.
  • Your API keys are stored only in the iOS Keychain on your device and are never uploaded.

Data storage & deletion

  • Data is stored on Supabase (servers in the AWS Tokyo region).
  • You can delete your flows, skills, documents, and posts in the app at any time.
  • To delete your account and all data, email us; we process within 30 days.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

Updates will be posted on this page; significant changes are announced in the app.
