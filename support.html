FAQ

Is my data private?
Yes. By default AI runs on-device and your content never leaves your phone. Your data is tied to an anonymous ID and only you can access it. See our Privacy Policy.

How does the free local model work?
TaptFlow ships with a built-in on-device model, so basic AI flows run offline, for free, with no account needed.

How do I delete my data?
Delete flows, skills, and documents anytime in the app, or email us to remove your account and all data (processed within 30 days).

Do I need to pay?
The core app and the on-device model are free. Paid plans (coming soon) unlock more powerful cloud models.
